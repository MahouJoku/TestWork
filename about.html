How to Play ARCS

Click here to go back home
  1. Firstly, the player with the initiative marker takes the first turn of a round
  2. They have to play a leading card and take a number of actions according to that card
  3. Then, everyone else in turn order attempts to surpass, pivot, or copy the lead card with one of their own card
  4. After everyone has played, end the round and see if initiative has been passed
  5. Discard all played cards and start a new round until everyone is out of cards
  6. When everyone is out of cards, score ambitions and check if someone has the neccessary power to end the game
  7. If not, start and new chapter, reshuffle the cards, and deal everyone six cards
Card Name Role Abilities
Mobilization Movement Can move ships around or influence the court
Administration Resources Can tax cities, repair ships, or also influence the court
Aggression Takeover Can battle other players, secure cards in the court, or also move ships
